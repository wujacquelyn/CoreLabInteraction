What You Need:

  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • a bit of flour
  • 9" cake pans x 3
  • Round sheet of Parchment Paper

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F. Liberally butter three 9” cake pans, and tap a tablespoon of flour around in each, until evenly coated. Line with a round sheet of parchment paper.
